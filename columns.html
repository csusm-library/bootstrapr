Columns

Use columns to structure your page content.

Although many more column combinations are possible, these column layouts are among the most common. Stack them atop each other to structure your page. Place your columns inside rows. You may place an unlimited number of columns inside a single row, with each horizontally aligned set of columns adding up to 12.
