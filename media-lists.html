Media Lists

Use media lists to organize combinations of visual and textual data.

Media lists pair images, links, and short descriptions. They are also well-suited to pages with several subpages, where they cam serve as a strongly visual table of contents.

× Formatting Possibilities
Images in media lists may be aligned to top, middle, or bottom. They may be aligned to the right and nested within other media lists. For more information on additional settings, see the Bootstrap documentation for more information.
