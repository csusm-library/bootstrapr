Google Embeds

Leverage the power of third party APIs from Google.

You may embed a Google Doc, Sheet, or Slide Presentation directly by using an iframe. Set the height of your iframe window based on how much of the document you want to show at once without scrolling.

Google Suite embeds HTML inside an <iframe> element. On iPads the iframe will show full-length and ignore any 'window height' property. Do not use this feature if such behavior will have an adverse effect on users.
